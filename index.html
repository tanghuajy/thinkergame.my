Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an exciting online game where you have to survive against hordes of monsters. Explore a vast and mysterious world, upgrade your weapons, and unlock new abilities to become the ultimate survivor.

Game Features

  • Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
  • Diverse range of monsters to fight
  • Upgradeable weapons and equipment
  • Unlockable skills and abilities

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move your character and the spacebar to attack. Collect coins and power-ups to enhance your performance. Good luck!
